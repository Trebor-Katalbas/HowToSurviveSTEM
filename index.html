ACADEMIC

Tips for surviving in academics. This involves setting goals, managing your time effectively, and staying motivated even in the face of setbacks and failures.

EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Maintaining a balance between your academic workload and your involvement in extracurricular activities.

SOCIAL LIFE

A healthy social life in academia can lead to a sense of belonging, increased motivation, and a more well-rounded educational experience.

PERSONAL LIFE

Setting boundaries, prioritizing self-care, and finding ways to incorporate personal interests and relationships into your academic routine.
